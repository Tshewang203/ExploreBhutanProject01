Discover Bhutan's Treasures

Explore iconic landmarks, sacred temples, and authentic cuisine

Historic Landmarks

Dochula Pass

A mountain pass featuring 108 memorial chortens and offering panoramic views of the Eastern Himalayas.

Memorial Chorten

A prominent landmark in Thimphu, this stupa is a center of daily worship for many Bhutanese.

Kyichu Lhakhang

One of Bhutan's oldest temples, built in the 7th century by Tibetan King Songtsen Gampo.

Sacred Sites & Valleys

Paro Taktsang

The iconic Tiger's Nest monastery, perched dramatically on a cliff 3,000 feet above the Paro valley. A spiritual and architectural marvel.

Punakha Dzong

The majestic fortress at the confluence of Mo Chhu and Pho Chhu rivers. Known as the Palace of Great Happiness.

Thimphu

The vibrant capital city, home to the iconic Buddha Dordenma statue and the only capital without traffic lights.

Bumthang Valley

The spiritual heartland of Bhutan, featuring ancient temples and monasteries in a stunning valley setting.

Phobjikha Valley

A glacial valley and winter home to endangered black-necked cranes. Perfect for nature lovers.

Haa Valley

One of Bhutan's most picturesque and least visited valleys, offering authentic cultural experiences.
